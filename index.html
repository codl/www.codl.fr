Personal projects

Ongoing

  • Miso is an effort to take care of every gripe I've had with music players and music library software. I've had a lot. Currently nowhere near completion.
  • DBGB is a Game Boy port of Desert Bus, a minigame from the unreleased Sega CD game Penn & Teller's Smoke and Mirrors. I am hoping to finish it before Desert Bus for Hope 8, make a working, official looking Game Boy cart, and send it to be auctioned for charity.

Derelict

  • EqBeats is a music publishing platform for ponies I've built with my friend fmang. It launched in December of 2011 and has seen steadily growing popularity and traffic since. It's now fairly stable, and neither of us is interested in working on it much anymore.
  • Autoplay (or autoplay.py) was my first try at fixing music playing software. It is a rudimentary radio mode for MPD that learns your taste from your playing history.
