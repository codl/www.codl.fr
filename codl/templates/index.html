Visuals

I draw and paint cartoon characters, digitally and traditionally. I've been called a furry artist, which should give you an idea, but I prefer to call myself an animal drawer. I also dabble in other kinds of visual arts like 3D modeling.

In the future I want to have an art gallery on this site, but until then you can find my art on donphan.social or DeviantArt. I also crosspost on Twitter and Tumblr.
