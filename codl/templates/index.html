Who's codl?

I'm codl! I thought I made that clear! I'm a queer weirdo from France in their late twenties. They/them, please.

It's pronounced "co-dell". Like Adele but, with co.

I call myself a dot-comrade, a computer toucher, and an animal drawist. I have interests in databases, the web, open source (but not in that way), decentralised systems (but not in that way), visual arts, music, cartography, video captioning, among other things.
